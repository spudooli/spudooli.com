Learning 3D printing

My father died on January 30th 2024. I've spent the last 3 months winding up his estate. It's still not done, just need to visit the bank with a bunch of forms completed and then the job is done

I inherited some of his things that I could actually fit into our lives. Some handtools, some tool cabinets he had made, a TV and an ampliier and a record player

I restored the amp enough to make it work again. After sitting in the attic for 25 years it needed some love. And contact cleaner sprayed into all the switches and pots more than love I guess

The record player has been sent off for restoration by someone that knows what they are doing

Playing with those devices precipitated me getting out my CD player which I haven't touched in 25 years.

Of which the cog that drives the CD drawer open failed

Which precipitated finding a solution - so we've just purhcased a 3d printer. It arrives in a couple of weeks

Now I'm learning 3d design and 3d printing

Painting the house

We've been in our house now for 14 years

The previous owners painted the house right before they sold it. And they did a terrible job of it, for the most part

I should have repainted the house years ago but I've been putting it off, not wanting to learn how to paint a house

But I've now started. Doing one wall a weekend, when the weather is fine. Going to pay someone to do the high parts though

It's no where near as fun as I thought it was not goig to be. I find myself looking forward to crappy weather in the weekends now.
